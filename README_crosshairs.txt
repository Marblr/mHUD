To configure custom crosshairs, navigate to 'scripts/crosshairs'.

KnucklesCrosses.png - crosshair legend.

crosshair_settings.res - crosshair type, size, outline, and position.

crosshair_colors.res - crosshair colors (requires restart when changed).

hudanimation_crosshair.txt - crosshair animations (advanced).

If the crosshairs do not work in-game, try installing or reinstalling the KnucklesCrosses.ttf font in the resource/fonts folder.
